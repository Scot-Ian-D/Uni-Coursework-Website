Spaceballs

IMDB: Spaceballs

Year Released: 1987

Based On: Original screenplay by Mel Brooks

Starring: Mel Brooks, John Candy, Rick Moranis, Bill Pullman

Studio: MGM

Quotes: "What's the matter, Colonel Sandurz? CHICKEN?", "No, no, no. Go past this. Pass this part. In fact, never play this again.", "You idiots! These are not them! These are their STUNT DOUBLES!"

About Pizza-the-Hutt: Not strictly a food, but a gangster made out of pizza. Lone Starr is in huge amounts of debt to Pizza-the-Hutt and is doing his best to avoid him whilst he tries to drum up the money to pay him off.

In the end, Pizza-the-Hutt meets his demise so Lone Starr manages to avoid having to pay him.

Trivia: President Skroob's name is an anagram of Mel Brooks, the man who plays him.

The box for Spaceballs: The Breakfast Cereal says it contains "100% sugar".

Mel Brooks tried to get either Tom Cruise or Tom Hanks for the role of Captain Lone Starr.

The bumper sticker on the back of Lone Starr's Winnebago spaceship reads: I [heart] URANUS.
