Back to the Future: Part II

IMDB: Back to the Future Part II

Year Released: 1989

Based On: Original screenplay by Bob Gale

Starring: Michael J. Fox, Christopher Lloyd, Billy Zane

Studio: Universal

Quotes: "Roads? Where we're going we don't need roads", "Shark still looks fake", "Great Scott!"

Dehydrated Pizza: In the future, Pizza Hut have developed the Dehydrated Pizza. In essence this is a normal pizza, just around a quarter of the size of a normal pizza and is sold in space-like foil packets

Once removed from the packing they are put into the Black and Decker Rehydrator and within 2 seconds they are rehydrated and increase to the size or a normal pizza and are cooked. No fuss. No hassle. No mess.

Trivia: First film appearance by Elijah Wood. He plays one of the two video game boys to whom Marty speaks in the Cafe 80's near the start of the film.

The ledge on the clock tower that Doc broke in Back to the Future (1985) is still broken in 2015.

According to the USA Today in the film, Marty McFly, Jr. is arrested for stealing an unsubstantiated amount of money from the Hill Valley Payroll Substation.

In the alternate timeline, George McFly is murdered on March 15, the Ides of March. This date was made infamous by the assassination of respected Roman leader Gaio Giulio Cesare (Julius Caesar) in 44 B.C.
